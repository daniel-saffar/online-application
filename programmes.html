Data Analytics & Artificial Intelligence

AT A GLANCE

The MSc in Data Analytics & Artificial Intelligence will hone your business decision making skills. You will acquire a “common language" understandable by data scientists, developers, and managers to create insightful analysis and innovative strategies for businesses in the digital age. The programme prepares you for a wide range of positions and opportunities where programming, quantitative analysis, critical thinking and business management skills are essential.

Language: English

Location: Lille Campus

Start Date: Early Sept-20

Deadline to apply: 30-Jun-20

LEARNING OUTCOMES

  • Apply your data analytics expertise to a wide range of industries
  • Acquire skills in programming and data science
  • Devise successful analytics strategies
  • Conduct Big Data projects and manage innovation
  • Master critical thinking tools and create successful, actionable and innovative strategies
  • Learn a new tech language
  • Communicate effectively and professionally, both in written and in oral communication
  • Demonstrate thinking and problem-solving skills applied to the intersection of data science, IT and managerial challenges
  • Understand economic, societal, business and ethical impacts of Artificial Intelligence

CERTIFICATES

> Le Wagon or Openclassrooms: Le Wagon is a career booster for business school students about to work in technological environments. This training will help you differentiate yourself from a more traditional profile when you are looking for Product Manager or Data Analyst positions. Le Wagon hands over all the tools you will need to develop a project or work hand in hand with a technical team and comprehend their mindset. As the saying goes "The code is the new alphabet", therefore learning how to code through Le Wagon Bootcamp is a great opportunity! To get certified by Openclassrooms, you will have to complete the full tracks (Data Analyst or Data Scientist).

> Google Analytics: Analytics Academy will teach you how to use Google's measurement tools to help you grow a business by implementing intelligent data collection and analysis methods. You can get individual certification.

> Challenge Le Tableau: EDHEC's MSc in Data Analytics & Artificial Intelligence gives students in-depth knowledge by mastering Tableau, the interactive data-visualization software and holds a challenge.
